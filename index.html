— Clubs —

Software Developer, Embedded Systems

McMaster Formula Electric SAE
Hamilton, ON

September 2016 - Current

I initially started in this club on the mechanical team, working to create steering wheel designs on SolidWorks and 3D printing prototypes. Now, I work on the software team, dealing with creating C programs for embedded systems. My current project utilizes an ATmega32M1 chip to interface with the race car’s CAN network and control fans through PWM. This will allow us to cool the race car while also varying fan speeds depending on temperature sensor readings, ultimately saving power. The plan for this team is to compete against other North American teams this summer, with the long-term goal of competing internationally in Europe.
